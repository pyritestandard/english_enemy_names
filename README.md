# English Enemy & Item Names

![Preview](https://github.com/pyritestandard/english_enemy_names/blob/main/preview.png?raw=true)

A simple language mod that replaces Noita’s Finnish names with a clear, faithful English translation.

All names are based on the Noita Wiki to keep them accurate and faithful to their original names. No jokes or creative liberties, just literal translations.

---

## What This Mod Changes

- Enemy Names 
- Unique Wands 
- Items
- Spells and Perks such as **Summon Beamstone** or **Summon Magic Wand**
- Twitch Integration Events
- And **More**

This is **only** a language file:
- It does **not** change gameplay.
- It does **not** trigger “mods enabled” restrictions.

## How to Enable the Language

![How to Change Language](https://github.com/pyritestandard/english_enemy_names/blob/main/images/How%20to%20Change.png?raw=true)

1. Open **Options** in Noita.
2. Go to the **General** tab.
3. Look at the bottom to the **Miscellaneous** section.
4. ___Right-click___ on **Language:** until you see  
   **`English [Translated Names]`**
5. Back out of the menu to apply.

You can switch back at any time by left-clicking the language again until you see **`English`**.
